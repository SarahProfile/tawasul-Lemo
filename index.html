Frequently Asked Questions

What is Tawasul Limousine for business?

This is a special service that makes it easy to manage and control all corporate taxi rides in your company.

+ What does it take to get connected?

Getting connected is simple. Contact our business team, provide your company details, and we'll set up your corporate account within 24-48 hours.

+ How can my employees order taxi?

Employees can order through our mobile app by selecting the corporate payment option, or you can book rides for them through our web interface.

+ What is your advantage for the company?

We offer transparent reporting, cost control, VAT deduction benefits, flexible employee settings, and complete trip transparency for better expense management.

+ What settings and limits can I enter in the system?

You can set vehicle class restrictions, approved pickup/drop-off locations, spending limits per employee, and specify allowed days/times for travel.

+ Is there any subscription fee for the product?

No, there are no subscription fees. You only pay for the rides your employees take, with transparent pricing and detailed billing.

+ Is there a possibility of deferred payment?

Yes, we offer flexible payment terms for corporate accounts, including monthly billing and credit arrangements based on your company's requirements.

+ Is VAT included in the cost of rides?

Yes, VAT is included in all ride costs. As a VAT-registered company, you can reclaim VAT on business travel expenses through our detailed invoicing.
